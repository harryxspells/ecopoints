About Ecopoints

Revolutionizing recycling through innovation and rewards

Our Mission

To create a sustainable future by making recycling accessible, rewarding, and engaging for everyone. We aim to significantly reduce plastic waste in our communities while incentivizing positive environmental behavior through smart technology and meaningful rewards.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every plastic bottle finds its way back into the recycling loop. Through our network of smart reverse vending machines and an intuitive mobile app, we're building a circular economy that benefits both the environment and our users.

Why It Matters

Plastic pollution is one of the most pressing environmental challenges of our time. Over 8 million tons of plastic enter our oceans annually. By creating an easy and rewarding recycling system, Ecopoints encourages responsible waste disposal, reduces landfill accumulation, and supports the recycling industry in creating new products from recycled materials.

Machine Technology

Advanced reverse vending machines powered by AI and IoT

Reverse Vending Machines

Our state-of-the-art reverse vending machines are designed for durability, efficiency, and user-friendliness. Each machine features a spacious deposit area, clear instructions, and a digital display that guides users through the recycling process. Built to withstand high-traffic usage, our machines operate 24/7 and require minimal maintenance.

  • Weather-resistant exterior suitable for indoor and outdoor installation
  • User-friendly touchscreen interface in multiple languages
  • Secure payment integration for instant point crediting
  • Automated bottle sorting and compacting technology

Smart Sensors

Advanced optical and weight sensors automatically detect, count, and categorize plastic bottles. Our AI-powered recognition system ensures accurate identification of bottle types and sizes, preventing fraud and ensuring fair point allocation.

Plastic Detection

Using near-infrared spectroscopy and machine learning algorithms, our system can distinguish between PET, HDPE, and other plastic types, ensuring proper sorting for efficient recycling processes.

Waste Collection Alerts

Integrated IoT sensors monitor fill levels in real-time. When capacity reaches 80%, automated alerts are sent to our collection team, ensuring machines are serviced promptly and remain available for users.

Cloud Connectivity

All machines are connected to our secure cloud platform, enabling real-time data synchronization, remote monitoring, and instant point crediting to user accounts.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Ecopoints

How do I get started with Ecopoints?

Getting started is easy! Simply download the Ecopoints mobile app from the App Store or Google Play, create an account, and you're ready to start recycling. Find a nearby machine using the app's location finder, scan the QR code at the machine, and start depositing your plastic bottles.

What types of plastic bottles can I recycle?

Our machines accept most plastic bottles, including PET and HDPE bottles (water bottles, soda bottles, juice bottles, etc.). The bottles should be empty and reasonably clean. Our smart sensors automatically detect and sort different types of plastic.

How many points do I earn per bottle?

Point values depend on bottle size: small bottles (500ml) earn 5 points, medium bottles (1L) earn 10 points, and large bottles (1.5L+) earn 15 points. You can also earn bonus multipliers for daily recycling streaks and special promotions!

How do I redeem my Ecopoints for rewards?

Open the Ecopoints app and browse the Rewards section. Select the reward you want, check if you have enough points, and tap "Redeem." You'll receive a digital voucher or code that you can use immediately or share with friends. Some rewards require partner app integration.

Where are Ecopoints machines located?

Ecopoints machines are strategically placed in high-traffic areas including shopping malls, supermarkets, parks, universities, transit stations, and office complexes. Use the app's map feature to find the nearest machine and see real-time availability status.

Do my points expire?

Your Ecopoints remain valid as long as your account is active and you recycle at least once every 12 months. Points from promotions may have specific expiration dates, which will be clearly communicated when you earn them.

What happens to the bottles I deposit?

All bottles collected through Ecopoints machines are sorted by type, cleaned, and sent to certified recycling facilities. The recycled plastic is then processed into new products, creating a circular economy. We maintain full transparency about our recycling partners and processes.

Is there a limit to how many bottles I can deposit?

There's no daily limit to the number of bottles you can deposit, but individual machines have capacity limits. If a machine is full, the app will direct you to the nearest available machine. We recommend checking machine availability in the app before visiting.

Can I use Ecopoints without the mobile app?

While the mobile app provides the best experience with features like point tracking, reward browsing, and machine finder, you can still use Ecopoints machines by scanning a QR code at the machine. However, we highly recommend downloading the app for full functionality.

How do I contact Ecopoints support?

You can reach our support team through the contact form on this website, email us at support@ecopoints.com, or use the in-app support feature. We typically respond within 24 hours. For urgent issues with machines, you can report them directly through the app.
